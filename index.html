The Goose Feeding Game:

There are currently two modes:
Standard & Flying
Standard mode has geese appearing at a set interval at random places on the screen.
Flying mode has geese spawn at the bottom center of the screen and move to the edges following random trajectories.
The Main Objective is to Feed The Geese. When a level is over and 50% of the geese have been fed the next level in that mode unlocks and is playable.
Each mode has 10 levels with each level getting progressively harder. Good luck!!
